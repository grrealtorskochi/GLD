Register Device for Alert

After installing the App (Assuming you have registered Telegram Account) contact @somenameBot and click start. Now you will receive a welcome message from the Bot. Then, send your Device Serial Number to the Bot. After verifying your device serial number, you will receive a confirmation message from the Bot.

Important!

Telegram BOT does not support multiple users (If you try to register again in another device/telegram account your old telegram account will stop receiving Alerts and new account will get Alerts)


Device Setup


Wifi Setup

After connecting the device to Power, Hotspot will be automatically enabled with SSID Gas Leakage Detector

Step 1:

  • Connect to the wifi network having SSID Gas Leakage Detector (this wifi network does not have password).

    • Step 2:
  • Open your browser and type http://192.168.4.1 in the address bar.

    • Step 3:
  • Available Network Names (SSID) will be shown in the webpage. Enter your network name (SSID) and Password, after entering the details click Submit Button.

    • Step 4:
  • Verify whether the Green LED is off (it might take a few seconds), if so then your wifi configuration is correct and the device is successfully connected to the Wifi.

    • Step 5 (Only if Green LED is still ON after step 4):
  • If Green LED is On even after ~30 seconds then it might be due to wrong configuration(SSID or Password). In this case you have to click reset button on the device and hotspot will restart, Now please follow the same procedure (ie; from step 1).
    Also verify that device is in Wifi Network converage.
